This is Jeopardy!

But with a slightly more personalized twist.

In this version of the game, you can filter the questions so that you can play the game with questions that are most interesting to you. You will be playing with real Jeopardy questions that have aired in 2023.

First, select a category group from the dropdown. When you decide, click "Start Game," and your first question will appear! This version of the game will show 10 questions, and your goal is to get as many points as possible.

After your game, you can choose to restart and pick your category again. For any reason, if you want to restart mid-game, refresh the page and pick your category again.

Happy playing!
